# Neutral evidence and independent selection method

## Evidence first

Neutral research is completed and frozen before any connection is proposed.
Its executable queries cover the paid product, FREE product, customer language,
objections, and purchase triggers independently. Query text that names a
ritual, moment, pairing, bridge, creative angle, or both offered products
together fails as `RESEARCH_CONTAMINATION`.

Customer-language records preserve exact retrieved wording. Product facts,
customer voice, objections, purchase triggers, and market context remain
separate record kinds. Research needs at least two source families.

## Isolated divergence

At least four generator sessions each create one materially different
connection. A candidate contains:

- one plain-English sales argument;
- a specific buyer situation;
- independent paid and FREE product desire;
- a direct transaction explanation;
- distinct product roles;
- a replacement/removal test;
- current product evidence and frozen research IDs; and
- at least two preserved customer-language IDs.

Generators do not see other candidates and do not score themselves. Duplicate
sessions, observed candidate IDs, generic AI glue, missing customer language,
or wording variants fail before criticism.

## Independent challenge

A critic session that generated no candidate reads the exact frozen research
hash and candidate-set hash. It scores every candidate from zero to five on:

1. paid-product desire;
2. FREE-product desire;
3. first-touch clarity;
4. buyer-situation specificity;
5. transaction credibility;
6. evidence fit;
7. claim integrity; and
8. creative distinctiveness.

The critic must also record a concrete pass/fail finding for forced pairing,
generic AI language, unsupported assumptions, weak paid desire, weak FREE
desire, interchangeability, and customer-voice fit. A plan to challenge later
is not an executed challenge.

Evidence fit, claim integrity, paid desire, FREE desire, first-touch clarity,
and transaction credibility are gates at four. The primary is the highest
scoring candidate that passes every challenge. The backup is the strongest
eligible candidate with a different sales argument and buyer situation.

## Writing boundary

The clean writer packet contains no candidate score, frame, query recipe,
critic session, generator session, relationship mode, or prior-product
fingerprint. The writer translates current product truth, customer language,
objections, purchase triggers, and the selected argument into fresh natural
copy.

Finished copy receives a SHA-256 and a separate copy-chief review. Only accepted
copy may be mapped to page sections, visible image text, image jobs, or prompts.
